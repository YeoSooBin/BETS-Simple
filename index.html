What is the Problem?

About 30 Strategic Planners in a public insittuition

need to go through a lot of material

to keep abreast of geopolitical, societal and technological trends.

They scan over 50 web-sources every month that use up ~20 man-hours every month.

This results in anxiety over missing out on processing key information

A typical User Journey without BETS

Officers visit webpages (5 manhours/mth)

Process info and extract what is relevant or new (10 manhours/mth)

Collate and publish as Newsletter (5 manhours/mth)

A good solution would reduce the workload by at least 5 man-hours per month (25%)

A Stretch goal is to increase the coverage of articles scanned by at least 20%.
