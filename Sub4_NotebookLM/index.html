NootebookLM is a Google experimental platform that applies Gen-AI technology to a repository and produces written products based on the knowledge in the repository. Unfortunately, Google has not released an API for her NotebookLM platform. When they do, we will try and use Google APIs to download the files discovered by Search and use NotebookLM to produce products and podcasts. See the samples below for a teaser of what is possible!
